March 27

On this day I hung out with Tanmoy and some of his friends. Tanmoy and I decided to meet up at Grand Central Station and then travel to Queens together from there. Once we got off the train at Mets Willis Station, we walked to the unisphere which is where everyone was meeting up. All of his friends were already there. We all chilled for a while and took some group photos. Then we found our way to Queens museum. Overall the museum seemed to have no point except for its panorama of the City of New York. It had the entire blueprint of New York City and there was such beautiful details. Then we walked over to Queens Zoo. The zoo was so fun, especially the petting zoo!! The animals were so cute and friendly. After the zoo, we went to get food which was about a 30 minute walk. We all ate food and then Tanmoy and I went to Kung Fu Tea for bubble tea. I had to leave early, around 5:30 because I had a 2 hour travel home but overall it was a really fun day!!

March 30

Tanmoy and I planned to have a food day this day. We met up at Penn Station at around 10:30. Our first food stop was at Rose Pizza and Pasta. Their pizza is amazing and probably the best in NYC and I really wanted Tanmoy to try it. But when we got there, we couldn't find Rose Pizza and when we asked, apparently they closed down. That was so sad. We exited the train station and found another pizza shop. We both got a buffalo slice and surprisingly it tasted just like Rose Pizza's and their shop had the same aesthetic so I think maybe they're the same workers. That pizza was really good. Then while we were there we exchanged light switch covers. I painted the one I had for him and then he gave me his blank one. He really liked the painting and that made me very happy 😊. Next we made our way to our next food stop which was Go Go Curry. We bought one meal and shared it. It was my first time trying it and it actually wasn't that bad. I liked it but it was loaded with salt. Then we walked to M&M world. Literally everything there has something to do with M&Ms. We each filled a bag with one of a kind M&Ms and bought it. Our next stop was Gong Cha where we bought a strawberry milk bubble tea and shared it. It actually tasted better than I expected. Next we walked to Central Park and had a picnic! We laid down a blanket and just chilled, talked, ate chips, kissed and laughed 😊. Our last stop was the Halal truck. We both got chicken kebabs and ate them on the steps of Columbus Circle. Once we were finished, we found our way to the train station and parted ways. It was such a fun day and I was genuinely happy. ❤
