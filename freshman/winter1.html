January 4 - January 22

These three weeks I participated in a winternship at Cognizant. Cognizant is a company that develops softwares and portals and sells them to health care and insurance companies. There was about 15 winterns in total and they split us up into groups of 3 or 4 to complete out project. My groups project was to create a help button and implement it in all of their links and forms. When the help button is pressed it reveals contextual help on how to fill out forms and what information is needed. This is most helpful for older users, those who are not technically advanced, and to provide defitions for medical terms. The first week of the winternship was mainly meetings, presentations and product demos. Each day we met people from different departments and had panel boards where we were able to ask questions. Later in the day for an hour, we would meet with the techical team and become familiar with their code. The second week was mainly the same but we spent three hours with the techical team and software developers. This was also the week that we started coding and testing things out. The third and final week was all coding, powerpoint making and practicing for the presentation. Overall, the 3 weeks was super stressful, busy and had a lot of sprint coding but I learned so much and gained a lot of experience in a professional coding environment.
