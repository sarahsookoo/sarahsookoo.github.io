December 26

Sharmin and I have a tradition where we hang out the day after Christmas. This year we created vision baords. We mainly looked at poem books because we were planning on doing a project with them for each other. Afterwards, we went back to Sharmin's house and ate lunch that her mom cooked. We chilled out for a bit again and then I was on my way back to a wifi-less home!
